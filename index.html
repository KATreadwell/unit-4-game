Crystal Collector!!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your score

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly-generated number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
